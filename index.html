EuroPolytech Student Enrolment Letter Generator

Bilingual enrolment declaration generator with print-to-PDF, download PDF, email draft, local save, and import/export tools.

The widget can generate the PDF and open a pre-written email draft, but standard browser security does not allow a normal HTML page to auto-attach a PDF directly into the user’s email window. This version gives a one-click draft plus PDF download workflow.
